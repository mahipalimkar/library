About VJTI Library

The library at VJTI provides numerous features and benefits to its users:

  • Access to Information: Our library provides access to a wide range of information and resources, catering to the needs and interests of students.
  • Research and Study: We offer a quiet and conducive environment for research, study, and contemplation. In particular, we support students, researchers, and faculty members with specialized collections and services.
  • Cultural Preservation: Plays a crucial role in preserving cultural heritage by collecting and safeguarding valuable documents, manuscripts etc.
  • Promotion of Literacy: Promotes literacy and lifelong learning by providing access to books and educational materials for people of all ages.
  • Community Engagement: Serves as community hubs, hosting events, workshops, and activities that bring people together and foster social interaction.

Library Rules

  • Cell phones: Cell phones today are a critical piece of staying in touch any where any time. Use be restricted to academic purposes or emergency. Either turn them off or set them to silent mode. Take any conversations — phone or in person — outside the Library.

  • LIBRARY TIMINGS: Policies on admittance, borrowing, hours, and services vary. Contact the librarian for details. VJTI Library Resources serve as an important research tool for VJTI’s current students, faculty, staff, and researchers who hold current VJTI ID CARD. Services change depending on staff levels. It is best to check. Important notices and announcements are always displayed, highlighted, or marked in RED. Please read them carefully even if you are in a hurry.

  • LENDING / READING HALL / E-RESOURCES TIME: In the READING HALL some text/reference books are kept for your reference/reading against your current VJTI ID. You may take your own books only in this part of the Library.

Feel free to contact the library staff if you have any questions regarding these rules. Visit our contact page for more information.
